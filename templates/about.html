About


Welcome to the Friendship Book. This is a place where my friends can introduce themselves by making a post. It is based on the idea of a traditional friendship book, where people write fun facts about themselves and answer questions.

Each friend can create one post, and they can update it whenever they want. A post includes:

  • A self-drawn portrait
  • Basic details like age, gender, and favorite color
  • Answers to a few interesting questions
